# Customer Churn Model

## Business Goal
Beta Bank has had customers leaving and would like to retain its customers. Therefore, Beta Bank seeks to determine the likelihood of a customer leaving the bank soon. Use data on clientsâ€™ past behavior and termination of contracts with the bank to build a model that can predict whether or not a client will leave based on client information. Aim for minimum F1 score of 0.59.

## Skills
pandas, matplotlib, seaborn, sklearn, machine learning, machine learning with imbalanced data

## Result
The RandomForestClassifier had an F1 score of 0.59, and accuracy score of 0.84, and AUC_ROC score of 0.82 on the test set.
